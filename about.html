About Akrivos

One developer. Niche markets. Focused software.

Akrivos is a one-person software company. I look for niche markets where people need better tools and nobody's building them. Then I build them.

The best software solves a specific problem for a specific group of people. That's it.

What I Build

Right now the focus is mobile apps - iOS and Android. I look for niche markets and either improve on what's already out there or build something new entirely. The first products lean toward education and skill-building, but the approach applies to any niche worth going after.

How I Work

I find a market that's underserved, spend time understanding what people in that space actually need, and then build something for them. No feature bloat. No trying to be a platform. Just a good tool that does its job.

Being a solo operation means I can move fast and stay focused on what matters. No roadmap driven by investor pressure or committee decisions. If something needs fixing, I fix it. If a feature doesn't make sense, it doesn't ship.

The Name

Akrivos comes from a Greek word meaning precise or exact. It seemed fitting.
